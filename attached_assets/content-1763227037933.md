Frequently asked questions

## Got questions?We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about transforming your practice with AI-powered documentation.

### How does it understand medicine so deeply?

Our proprietary medical reasoning engine goes beyond pattern matching. It contextualizes patient histories, cross-references symptoms with the latest research, and identifies connections that might take hours of manual review. Think of it as having the collective knowledge of thousands of specialists, instantly available.

### Is my patient data secure and HIPAA compliant?

Yes, absolutely. We're fully HIPAA compliant with end-to-end encryption, zero data retention after processing, and SOC 2 Type II certification. Your data never trains our models, and all processing happens in secure, isolated environments.

### What makes this different from other medical AI?

While others focus on simple transcription or basic suggestions, we've built something that truly understands medical context. Our engine processes entire patient histories, identifies rare conditions, and provides evidence-based recommendations—all while learning from the latest medical literature in real-time.

### Does it integrate with my existing EHR?

Yes, we support all major EHRs through FHIR R4/R5 standards. Export notes directly or use our API for seamless integration. We also provide copy-paste formatted outputs for any system.

### What if I need to modify the AI's suggestions?

You have complete control. Every suggestion is editable before approval. The AI learns from your preferences over time, adapting to your documentation style and clinical patterns.

### Can it really reduce misdiagnosis?

Our pattern recognition has shown a 50% reduction in diagnostic errors in early testing. By analyzing complete patient histories and cross-referencing with millions of cases, it catches rare conditions and drug interactions that are easy to miss during busy clinical days.

### How does it integrate with existing workflows?

We designed this to enhance—not disrupt—your current workflow. It works alongside your EHR, processes data in real-time, and provides insights exactly when you need them. Think of it as an incredibly knowledgeable colleague who's always available.

### What's the vision for the future?

We're building towards a future where no diagnosis is missed, where treatment plans are optimized for each patient's unique history, and where physicians can focus on patient care instead of documentation. This demo is just the beginning of transforming medicine as we know it.
